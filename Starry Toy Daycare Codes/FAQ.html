FAQ Section

What is the Starry Toy Daycare?

The Starry Toy Daycare is a place for kids to keep their toys and plushies in one place. The objectiive is to have kids keep their beloved toys in one place, whether they have it currently or not.

Q&A Home Page

How do i get to the Zoo or the Playhouse?

The map has specific places the click to get to the different areas around the Daycare. Click the clouds or for less specific:
Zoo: Click the bear,the ballpit, or the 2 shelves.
Shopping Mall: Click the kitchen, the shopping carts, or the cashier counter.
Playground: The whole playground set is clickable.
PlayHouse: The whole house is clickable.
Friends: Click the Rhino, the dinosaur on the red bean bag chair, or the dinosaur next to it.

How do I go back to the first page?

To get back to the "Landing page" or the very first page, click the logo located at the top of the screen. To get back to the "Home page" on any other page, click the green arrow button on the left side of the screen.

Q&A Zoo

What is a Starrling?

A Starrling is your toy or pet. It is a name that is given to all your toys for reference in the zoo.

Why are there 3 plushies in my zoo already?

In like most games or websites, you are given starter items. In the Starrling Zoo, you are given 3 starter Shiba Plushies.

How do I enter the description for my Starrling?

Click on the Starrling of your choice and a popup window will appear. Then you will be able to enter any text for your Starrling. You will be able to enter their name, birthday, and a description.

Why can't I add my toy into the zoo?

Signing in or signing up is currently unavailable. You are under a guest sign in, which addig your own toys is not available to the guest users as it wouldn't save.

Q&A Shopping Mall

How do I buy the furniture and wallpapers?

Click the "Buy" button under the furniture or wallpaper of your choice.

How do I preview the wallpapers?

Click on the wallpaper that you want to preview and it will show you a preview of the photo in a seperate tab

Q&A Playground

How do I move my toys onto the playground?

Click and drag any of the toys and move them onto the playground anywhere you want.

Q&A Playhouse

How do I move my toys into the room?

Click and drag any of the toys and move them onto the playhouse anywhere you want.

How do I move my furniture into the room?

Click and drag any of the furniture and move them onto the playhouse anywhere you want.

How do I change the background of the room?

To change the room, click the arrows on either the left or the right side. On default, you will be able to change between 6 different backgrounds.

Q&A Friends

How do I add friends?

Click the "Add Friends" button on the top right corner. You are given a random code on guest sign in and a set on upon login in. Add your friends code and click enter. If you enter in nothing or the wrong code. You will end up with a error message.

Please note that adding friends is currently unavailable at the moment.
