"Run and play checkers"


Download Visual Studio
Open up files, "Checkers.html, Checkers.css, and Checkers.js"
Click "Run" and click "Start Debugging" 
Pick preferred Web App Chrome or Web App Edge.

Click on tan or brown first and move it one space to the right or left forward. Continue back and forth between each color until you get 
one color on the opposing side.

Note-
#1 Rules are not applied in code, but keep to the rules of the game
#2 Checker pieces are not removed after a piece has been crossed over it
